FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

How long does it take to set up an automation?

Most automations go live in 3-7 days. Simple workflows (like lead capture or email follow-ups) can be done in 1-2 days. Complex systems with multiple integrations take 1-2 weeks.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

No. We handle everything - setup, testing, and training. You just tell us what you want automated, and we build it. You'll get a simple dashboard to monitor everything.

What does it cost?

It depends on the complexity and scope. Simple automations cost less, while complete systems with multiple integrations are a larger investment. We'll give you a clear quote after a quick discovery call - no hidden fees.

Will it work with my existing tools?

Yes. We integrate with 500+ tools - Google Sheets, WhatsApp, CRMs, accounting software, payment gateways, and more. If your tools have an API, we can connect them.

What if something breaks?

We monitor your automations and fix issues proactively. You get free support after launch, and we offer ongoing maintenance plans if you want continued monitoring and updates.

Can I see it before paying?

Yes. We'll show you a demo or prototype before you commit. No surprises.

What's the ROI? Is it worth it?

Most clients save 10-20 hours per week. The automation typically pays for itself in the first month through time saved and leads captured.

I'm not sure what to automate. Can you help?

Absolutely. Book a free 15-minute call. We'll look at your current processes and tell you exactly where automation will have the biggest impact.
