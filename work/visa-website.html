VISA SERVICES WEBSITE

Times of Visa needed a website that loads fast, ranks well on Google, and converts visitors into leads. We built them a clean, professional site that scores 90+ on PageSpeed and started generating organic leads within weeks.

THE CHALLENGE

The existing website had multiple problems:

  • Slow loading (5+ seconds on mobile) — visitors left before it loaded
  • Not mobile-friendly — 70% of their traffic was mobile
  • Poor SEO — not ranking for any relevant keywords
  • No clear conversion path — visitors didn't know how to inquire
  • Outdated design — didn't inspire trust for visa services

THE SOLUTION

1. Speed-First Design: We rebuilt the site from scratch with performance as the priority. Optimized images, minimal JavaScript, fast hosting. Result: loads in under 2 seconds.

2. Mobile-First Layout: Designed for phones first, then scaled up. Clean, readable, with thumb-friendly buttons and forms.

3. SEO Foundation: Proper page structure, meta tags, schema markup, and content optimized for "visa services + [country]" keywords.

4. Clear CTAs: Every page has a clear next step — WhatsApp button, inquiry form, or call button always visible.

5. Trust Signals: Added testimonials, success stories, and clear process explanation to build confidence.
