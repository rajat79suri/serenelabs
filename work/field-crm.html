FIELD SALES TRACKING SYSTEM

A distribution company needed to track their 15-person field sales team without spending months on a complex CRM implementation. We built a lightweight PWA that went live in 3 days — no app store needed.

THE CHALLENGE

The sales manager had no visibility into what her team was doing in the field:

  • Reps filled out paper forms that got typed into Excel at night
  • No way to verify if visits actually happened
  • Follow-ups were tracked in personal notebooks — and often forgotten
  • Daily reports took 2-3 hours to compile manually
  • They'd tried traditional CRMs but the complexity killed adoption

THE SOLUTION

We built a Progressive Web App (PWA) — a web-based app that works like a native app but doesn't require any app store download. Reps just open a link and "add to home screen."

For Sales Reps:

  • One-tap check-in with GPS and timestamp
  • Quick visit form (60 seconds to complete)
  • Camera for business cards and site photos
  • Offline mode — works without internet, syncs when connected
  • Follow-up reminders pushed to their phone

For Managers:

  • Live map showing where each rep is
  • Daily visit counts and outcomes by rep
  • Overdue follow-ups highlighted
  • Auto-generated daily reports by email
  • Export to Excel anytime

THE RESULTS

Live in 3 days — from kickoff to full deployment with 15 users.

  • 100% adoption — because it's simple and fast
  • 80% reduction in admin time — no more manual data entry
  • Real-time visibility — managers see visits as they happen
  • Follow-up completion rate up 2x — because reminders actually work
  • No training needed — reps figured it out in 5 minutes
  • Works on any phone — Android, iPhone, even older devices

WHY PWA?

We chose a Progressive Web App over a native app because:

  • No app store approval — deploy updates instantly
  • No downloads — just share a link
  • Works offline — critical for field work
  • Cheaper to build — one codebase for all devices
  • Faster to deploy — 3 days vs. 3 months
