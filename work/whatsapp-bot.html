Booking Confirmation Automation

A travel agency was spending 3+ hours daily sending booking confirmations, payment receipts, and trip reminders manually on WhatsApp. We automated the entire flow - now it happens instantly, 24/7.

The Challenge

TripNest Holidays handles 30-50 bookings per day during peak season. Every booking required:

  • A confirmation message with trip details
  • Payment receipt after each installment
  • Document checklist before the trip
  • Reminders 7 days, 3 days, and 1 day before departure
  • Emergency contact details and travel tips

All of this was done manually by copying and pasting into WhatsApp. Staff was spending 3-4 hours daily just on messaging - time that could have been spent on sales.

The Solution

We built an automated WhatsApp messaging system triggered by their booking software:

1. Instant Confirmation: The moment a booking is confirmed in their system, the customer receives a beautifully formatted WhatsApp message with all trip details, within 30 seconds.

2. Payment Receipts: When a payment is recorded, an automatic receipt is sent with amount, balance due, and next payment date.

3. Pre-Trip Sequence: Automated messages go out at 7 days (document checklist), 3 days (packing tips), and 1 day (emergency contacts and final reminders).

4. Post-Trip Follow-up: A feedback request is sent 2 days after the trip, with a link to leave a Google review.

The Results

3+ hours saved daily - messaging that used to take half the day now happens automatically.

  • Instant response time - confirmations sent within 30 seconds, not hours
  • Zero missed reminders - every customer gets the right message at the right time
  • 35% increase in Google reviews - automated post-trip follow-ups actually get responses
  • Happier customers - they feel informed and cared for throughout their journey
  • Staff focused on selling - not copy-pasting messages all day

Customer Feedback

Within the first month, the owner noticed a change in customer calls:

"Before, customers would call asking 'Did you receive my payment?' or 'What documents do I need?' Now they get everything instantly on WhatsApp. Our support calls dropped by 60%. Customers are happier, and my team has time to focus on sales."
